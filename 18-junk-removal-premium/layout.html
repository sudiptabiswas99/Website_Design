We price by how much room your stuff takes up in the truck, from an eighth of a load up to a full truck. Labor, fuel, and dump fees are already included. You get the flat price before we load anything, so there's no surprise at the end. A few items like mattresses and tires carry a small added fee, and we tell you that up front.

Often, yes. Call or book before noon and we'll usually fit you in the same day, depending on how full the schedule is. Otherwise next-day is almost always open. We give you a two-hour arrival window and text you when the crew is on the way.

You don't lift a thing. Point at what goes and the crew does the carrying, loading, and cleanup. You don't have to be home either, as long as the items are accessible and you've confirmed the price with us first. Many customers leave things in the driveway or an unlocked garage.

We take almost any household or office item, but the law keeps us from hauling hazardous material: wet paint and solvents, chemicals and pesticides, asbestos, propane tanks, motor oil and fuel, and medical waste. We sort it before it ever reaches a landfill. Usable furniture goes to local charities, and metal and electronics go to recyclers. Yes. HaulAway is licensed and carries liability insurance, so your home and our crew are covered while we work. If you'd like to see proof before we start, just ask and we'll show you.
