Straight answers

FAQ

Still stuck? A real person answers the phone, day or night.

Do you charge for estimates?
No. Diagnostic visits come with a free, flat-rate quote you approve before any work starts. Emergency dispatch has a standard trip fee that's credited toward the repair.
Are you really available 24/7?
Yes, a live dispatcher answers around the clock and an on-call crew is always rolling. Burst pipes don't wait for business hours, so neither do we.
Are you licensed and insured?
Fully. We carry Colorado Master Plumber licensing plus full liability and workers' comp insurance. Certificates available on request.
What areas do you cover?
The greater Denver metro and Front Range: Denver, Aurora, Lakewood, Arvada, Centennial, Boulder, Westminster, and Thornton. Send your address through the form to confirm.
Is your work guaranteed?
Every repair carries a written workmanship warranty, and we honor manufacturer warranties on parts and fixtures we install. If it's not right, we come back at no charge.
