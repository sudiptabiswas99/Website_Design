Good questions

Frequently asked

Still wondering something? Call us — a real person answers, 24/7.

Do you charge for estimates?
No. Diagnostic visits come with a free, flat-rate quote you approve before any work begins. For emergencies there's a standard trip fee that's credited toward the repair.
Are you really available 24/7?
Yes — burst pipes and sewage backups don't keep business hours and neither do we. Call any time and a live dispatcher will get a licensed tech rolling to you.
Is BlueLine licensed and insured?
Fully. We carry Texas Master Plumber license #41872 and full liability plus workers' comp insurance. We'll happily share certificates on request.
What areas do you serve?
Austin and the surrounding metro — Round Rock, Cedar Park, Pflugerville, Georgetown, and Leander. Not sure if you're in range? Send your address through the quote form and we'll confirm.
Do you warranty your work?
Every repair is backed by a written workmanship warranty, and we honor all manufacturer warranties on the parts and fixtures we install. If something isn't right, we come back at no charge.
