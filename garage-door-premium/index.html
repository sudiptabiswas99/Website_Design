Broken spring

If you heard a loud bang and now it will not lift, it is the spring.

The spring carries the whole weight of the door. When it snaps the opener cannot lift it, and forcing it is how doors and openers get wrecked. Do not pull on it. Call us and we replace it the same day.

(562) 431-1960

A spring is under high tension. Replacing it is not a safe do-it-yourself job. Let a technician handle it.

Signs your spring is gone

  • A loud bang from the garage, then the door will not open
  • The opener runs and strains but the door barely moves
  • A visible gap or a hanging piece in the spring above the door
  • The door slams down hard or feels far heavier than usual by hand
