Good questions

Answers before you pick up the phone.

The things Sacramento homeowners ask us most. Don't see yours? Call and a real person answers.

01 Do you really give a price before you start?
Yes. You get a flat-rate quote up front and you approve it before we lift a wrench. The number we say is the number you pay — parts, labor, cleanup included. No hourly meter, no surprise line items.
02 Are you available nights and weekends?
We answer 24/7 for true emergencies — burst pipes, no water, sewage backups. And we don't charge overtime: a 2 AM Sunday call costs the same flat rate as a Tuesday afternoon.
03 How fast can someone get to me?
For emergencies across Greater Sacramento our average arrival is under 60 minutes. For everything else we set a visit window that works for your day — and we text when the plumber is on the way.
04 Is the work guaranteed?
Every repair and install carries a two-year warranty on both parts and labor. If something isn't right, one call and we're back to make it right — no charge.
05 Can I finance a big repair?
Yes. Repipes, water heaters, sewer lines and remodels can be split into monthly payments. Apply in minutes with a soft credit check that won't ding your score, with $0-down options and nothing due before we start.
06 Are you licensed and insured?
Fully. We're California C-36 licensed (#784512), bonded and insured, and BBB A+ accredited. Every plumber we send is in-house, background-checked, and drug-tested — never a day-labor sub.
07 What areas do you cover?
Sacramento, Roseville, Folsom, Elk Grove, Citrus Heights, Rocklin and the surrounding towns. Not sure about your block? Type your ZIP up top or browse our service areas — we'll tell you straight.
