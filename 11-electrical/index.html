07 / FAQ

Questions homeowners ask us.

A breaker that trips now and then is doing its job. One that trips over and over is telling you a circuit is overloaded or there is a fault, and it should be looked at. If you ever smell burning or see scorching at the panel, shut off the main and call the emergency line.

If you are adding an EV charger, a heat pump, or a hot tub, an older 100-amp panel often does not have the capacity. We check the actual load before we recommend anything, so you only upsize the service if the house needs it.
